Tester Text

For an italic text, we use "em" tag. The full form of it is "Emphasis element"

To underline words, we use "u" tag.

To make a word bold, we use "strong" tag.

To draw a line through the middle of a text (strikethrough), we use "s" tag.

To draw a horizontal line between the lines, we use "hr" tag which doesn't need a closure tag.

To adjust the background-color of a text, we use "background-color: rgba()" tag.
